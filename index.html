Music and the Middlebrow is an interdisciplinary, transnational research project exploring the relationship between musical culture and the middlebrow.

The project aims both to explore how the distinctive characteristics of musical culture might deepen our understanding of the middlebrow, as well as to use the middlebrow to intervene in current debates within musicology.

Over a three-year period we will bring together scholars from a range of disciplinary backgrounds in a series of discussion-focused events, with a view to fostering dialogue and facilitating the exchange of ideas.
