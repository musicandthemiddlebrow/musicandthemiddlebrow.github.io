We welcome proposals for individual papers (20 minutes) and panel sessions (3 papers) that respond to the project rationale.

Possible topics include, but are not limited to, the relationship between music, the middlebrow and:

  • Modernism / modernity
  • Popular culture
  • National identity
  • Film
  • Theater
  • Cultural geography
  • Religion
  • Politics
  • Social class
  • Race
  • Gender

Abstracts of around 300 words should be submitted to Kate Guthrie by 15 December 2016. Panel proposals should also include a brief (ca.150 word) statement explaining the broader rationale for the session.
